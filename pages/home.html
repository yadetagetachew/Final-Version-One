"Custom Shoes"

"Kicks" is an artist collective led by Yadi. Each pair of shoes is a custom 1 of 1, crafted by our team of skilled artists. We welcome any and all design challenges that you can come up with! Our shoes are sold exclusively to you and we will not be recreated, your ideas are safe with us.

Quality

Paying attention to detail and using Architectural design thinking we will make your kicks engineered to last. We take pride in customization with the vision of perfection, using top of the line materials to make you stand out. Just like an art piece, it is made with precision and assembled with craftsmanship, and that is the mindset we carry as we survive our clients.

Artistry

Our collective is a talented family of artists at Kicks. Some have been in the Fashion industry for seven plus years, with a talent and an eye for the next upcoming trend. Working for a true passion for the fashion industry they have proven their creative skill through countless projects that portray their creative ability while being Committed to breaking barriers and providing a piece of art to our clients.

Story

We all have a story, none of us have the same exact personality. So why do we have to have the same shoes. The fashion industry has made us all cookie cutters, looking the same. The ability to express yourself through fashion is priceless and we believe we are all creative. Our clients have a blank canvas to bring thier vision to life as we help them express to the world who they are and share a piece of yourself through kicks

How We Do It!

  • Stand Out

    Enjoy the shoes of your dreams. Your unique kicks will set you apart, as you continue to be the star you always were.
